About

This demo runs four speech tasks with a shared UI. The Emotion task uses HuBERT-large embeddings and an SVM classifier achieving 79.14% accuracy. Other tasks are wired and ready for model integration.

  • Emotion: HuBERT-large + SVM (79.14% accuracy, 5-fold CV)
  • CPU-friendly; first emotion run may download HuBERT-large weights (~1.2GB).
  • Models and preprocessors loaded from /models.
  • Pages: Emotion, Gender, Intent, Speaker.

Built with Flask + Bootstrap. Based on IEEE/ACM 2024 research.
